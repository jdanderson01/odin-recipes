Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is a staple at any family gathering that involves food, especially in the South! This recipe will show you how to make the perfect mac and cheese to compliment your soul food!

  • 8-10 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 (12-ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 lightly beaten eggs
  • 1 and a half teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 and a half teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 half teaspoon salt
  • 1 stick of butter cut into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 1 half pound Velveeta cut into small chunks
  • 8 ounces shredded Colby-jack cheese
  • 4 ounces shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mild cheddar cheese

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350ยบ. Bring large pot of water to boil and then salt generously. Cook macaroni just shy of package directions, al dente. Drain well and place in 9" by 13" baking dish or pan.
  • In same pasta pot, whisk together whole milk, evaporated milk, eggs, salt, pepper and sugar until combined. Pour mixture over cooked macaroni in pan.
  • In same pasta pot, whisk together whole milk, evaporated milk, eggs, salt, pepper and sugar until combined. Pour mixture over cooked macaroni in pan.
  • Bake for 35 to 40 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top. Let rest 10 minutes or so before serving. Enjoy!
